Insights from Sales Dashboard

1. Monthly Sales Trend – Sales increased steadily and peaked in November–December, reflecting strong year-end demand.

2. Regional Performance – The West region led with the highest sales, while the Central region recorded the lowest.

3. Category Contribution – Technology contributed the largest share of sales, followed by Furniture and Office Supplies.

4. Profitability Insight – Although sales were higher in the West, the South region showed better profit margins, highlighting efficiency in sales operations.
